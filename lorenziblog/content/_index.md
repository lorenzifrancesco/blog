    <h3>About me</h3>
    <p>I am an <strong>information engineer</strong> with a doctorate in <strong>theoretical condensed matter physics</strong>.</p>
    <p>I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in photonics and electromagnetics at the Department of Information Engineering of the University of Padova.</p>
    <p>I am mostly interested in describing wave phenomena in photonic and quantum systems. Some of my specific interests include:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Nonlinear wave propagation</li>
      <li>Theoretical photonics and electromagnetism</li>
      <li>Stochastic modeling for optical communications</li>
      <li>Ultracold quantum gases</li>
    </ul>
